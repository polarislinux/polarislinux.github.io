Why Polaris Linux?

Lodestar Desktop

Experience our custom desktop environment designed for clarity and efficiency. With intuitive navigation and a stunning dark theme, Lodestar guides you seamlessly through your workflow.

Stability & Security

Built on the rock-solid foundation of Linux Mint, Polaris Linux offers unmatched stability. Regular updates and built-in security features keep your system safe and reliable.

Cosmic Design

Immerse yourself in our carefully crafted cosmic theme. From the starry background to the gold-accented interface, every element is designed for visual comfort during long sessions.

Ready to Work

Comes with a curated selection of essential applications for productivity, creativity, and entertainment. Everything you need is just a click away.

Optimized Performance

Lightweight yet powerful, Polaris Linux runs smoothly on both modern hardware and older machines. Enjoy responsive performance without unnecessary bloat.

Community Driven

Join our growing community of users and contributors. Get help, share ideas, and help shape the future of Polaris Linux through forums and GitHub discussions.

Installation Instructions

1. Download the ISO file and verify its checksum
2. Create a bootable USB using balenaEtcher or Rufus
3. Boot from the USB and select "Install Polaris Linux"
4. Follow the graphical installer (takes 10-20 minutes)
5. Reboot and enjoy your new system!
